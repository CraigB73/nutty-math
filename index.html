Instructions

Hello, and welcome to Nutty Math.

It's not a challenge under five question so make sure to choose your challenge between 5-20 math questions.

Next, press the "Play Game" button and help Nutty race against the clock to gather acorns and boost your math skills!

Object of the game

Solve each math question, and for every correct answer, Nutty drops an acorn in your basket. Don't worry about a time limit because you can't collect an acron unless you find one. So to get an acorn you need to solve the math problem.

Happy Gathering!
